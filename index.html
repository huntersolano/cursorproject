Safety First

Essential checklist for a safe and enjoyable experience

Pre-Adventure Safety Checklist

Please review and check off these important safety items before your visit:

Check weather conditions before arrival
Bring sunscreen and sun protection (hat, sunglasses)
Wear appropriate water shoes or sandals
Pack water and snacks
Bring a change of clothes in a waterproof bag
Review basic water safety guidelines
Inform someone about your kayaking plans
